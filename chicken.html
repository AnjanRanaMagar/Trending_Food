Nepali Chicken Chilli

A famous spicy and sweet chicken dish in Nepalese authentic style. This recipe includes step by step guide with video and important notes for perfection.

Some of the Ingredients includes:

    500 grams Boneless chicken / 1 big sized Capsicum (green peppers) cut into medium-sized squares / 1 big sized Red onion separated layers and cut into halves / 2 medium sized Tomatoes cut into large cubes / 1 tablespoon Ginger garlic paste / 2 teaspoons Red chili powder / 2 tablespoons Tomato ketchup / 2 tablespoons Soy Saunce / 2 tablespoons corn starch and salt / 1/5 Green onions and 2 1/5 tablespoons vegetable oil /

Given below are some steps to cook the recipes.

    1. Heat up 2 tablespoons of oil.

    2. Mix chicken with corn starch.

    3. Fry the chicken in oil until somewhat crispy. Remove from the oil.

    4. In the same pan, add remaining oil, make it hot.

      a. Add ginger and garlic paste. Saute until brown.
      b. Add chicken, capsicum and red onion. Saute in high heat for 1 minute.

    5. Add soy sauce, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until sauce fully coated for just about 2 minutes.

    6. Add tomatoes and cook for another 15-20 seconds while stirring. Turn off the heat.

    7. Serve immediately, optionally garnish with green onions.
